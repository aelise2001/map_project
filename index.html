America Runs on Dunkin': or Does It?

Unraveling America's Coffee Tapestry by State.

By Tiana McGee

December 20, 2023

Credit:https://ny.racked.com/2014/12/23/7563339/chain-stores-nyc

In the diverse landscapes of America, the question lingers: Does the country truly run on Dunkin'? A look at Dunkin' locations across states shows a variety of coffee cultures.

Even after dropping the iconic "Donuts" in their name, the franchise remains on top on the East Coast. In a study done at Virginia Tech in 2020, it was said that “The findings demonstrated that social identification decreased slightly through the visual rebranding, but did not create any reported impact on purchasing habits". Three years later, Dunkin' remains ever-present. From Massachusetts with 1,075 Dunkin’ locations to Florida with 871 and the ever-caffeinated New York with 1,419 , it is evident that the East Coast is all about that pink and orange. Yet, venture to the landscapes of Vermont, where there are 49 locations, or Wyoming, where a single Dunkin' remains, and a different narrative unfolds. The West, it seems, approaches the Dunkin' experience with a different eye. As the data maps out Dunkin's presence or absence across states, the question of whether America truly runs on Dunkin' becomes a nuanced exploration of regional preferences and coffee loyalties. The answer, much like the vast diversity of the nation, is like a mosaic painted with the hues of coffee shop culture.
